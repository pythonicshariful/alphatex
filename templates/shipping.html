Return Policy

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original condition.

1

Contact Us First

Email or call us within 7 days of receiving your order with your order ID and reason for return.

2

Pack the Item

Pack the item securely in its original packaging with tags attached. Include your order details inside.

3

We Arrange Pickup

Our courier will collect the parcel from your address at no cost to you (inside Dhaka).

4

Exchange or Refund

Once we inspect and approve the return, your exchange or store credit will be processed within 2–3 business days.

Not eligible for return: Sale / discounted items, items without original tags, items showing signs of use or washing, underwear and socks (for hygiene reasons).
