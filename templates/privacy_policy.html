Data We Collect

When you use alphatex, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password (hashed) when you register.
  • Order information: Delivery address, phone number, and order history.
  • Browsing data: IP address, pages visited, products viewed, and session duration.
  • Device data: Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution.
  • Communications: Messages you send us via the contact form.

How We Use It

We use your data solely to deliver and improve our services:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders.
  • Sending order confirmations and delivery updates via phone.
  • Personalising your homepage with products relevant to your interests.
  • Improving our website, product listings, and user experience.
  • Preventing fraud and maintaining security.
  • Responding to customer support queries.

We do not use your data for unsolicited marketing without your explicit consent.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Delivery partners (name, address, phone) solely to fulfil your order.
  • Payment processors if digital payments are introduced in future.
  • Legal authorities if required by law or a valid court order.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your cart. We also use anonymous analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site. No tracking cookies are shared with advertisers. See our Cookie Policy for details.

Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose it was collected for:

  • Account data: until you request deletion.
  • Order records: 5 years (for legal and tax purposes).
  • Browsing/IP logs: 12 months.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Object to how we process your data.
  • Export your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@alphatex.com.

Security

All passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing. Our website uses HTTPS encryption. While we implement robust safeguards, no system is 100% secure — we recommend using a strong, unique password for your account.
